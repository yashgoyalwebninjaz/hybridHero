Security Management Solutions

  • Welcoming security protocols: Uphold workplace security in a friendly manner, ensuring the safety of people and data.

Compliance Assured

PUphold data protection & meet regulatory needs

Enterprise data security and compliance which reduce liability while simplifying audits..

  • Manage personal data securely: Take control of data storage and automatically keep a digital HR record of every employee and log every visitor that arrives.
  • Adhere to Global Regulations: Customise sign-in processes to gather necessary visitor information, needed to comply with GDPR, and other regulations.
  • Unified records: Comprehensive digital record of everything that occurs in your workforce - people, places spaces - under a single platform.

Health & Safety

Ensure a safe & productive work environment

Rely on seamless, automated technology to safeguard your workplace and keep your business running.

  • Health verification: Confirm vaccination statuses, set capacity limits, and enable employees to reserve desks as needed.
  • Safe sign-in: Touchless sign-in, real-time contact tracing, and provide a safe environment for your employees.
  • Automated Safety Protocols:Implement company-wide health and safety guidelines across all locations while safeguarding personal identifiable information.

Scalability

Optimise global operations & reduce costs

Monitoring locations, deploying to new sites, and standardising procedures are all made easier with centralised workplace management.

  • Reduce costs on real estate and business operations: Get consolidated data on occupancy, capacity, and space use so you can make intelligent decisions about your workspaces and staff.
  • Seamless integration: Easily with your existing tools, allowing you to get more done with them.
  • Customisable at scale: Utilise extensive features to craft brand-centric experiences, from global holiday calendars fostering belonging to consistent operations across sites, tailoring each interaction.
